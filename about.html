about holly

I am currently a junior at The University of Oregon and studying Sociology with a minor in Business Administration. At UO I work with the Nontraditional Student Program, where I create digital content to advertise evenst and resources. I have always had a passion for student engegement, participating in leadership classes and student engagement at my past schools. Being a nontraditional student myself, having taken a break from school and trasnfered to UO, I am glad to work for a department that specifically represents often underepresented students such as student parents, veterans, and trasnfer students. In my future I plan to continue to pursue a career in student engagement at a university level.


In my free time, I enjoy being outside and spending time with my friends and family. My boyfriend Ben and I love to explore around our home state of Oregon whenever we can, often visiting Portland and the coast. I also love to go "home" whenever I have time to -- home being my family's farm where my younger sisiter Heidi currently lives.


I've been interested in design for as long as I can remember, and like to have a creative outlet on my tumblr blog onlineholly.com, as well as on my Instagram and Pinterest. I am currently planning to start a podcast this summer as well.
